Sales Funnel Conversion

Tools Used: Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables & Charts)

In this project, raw lead data was transformed into a story of how prospects move through a sales pipeline. Out of 1,997 leads, only 150 deals closed — just 8% conversion. The biggest leak happened after proposals were sent, showing that the challenge wasn’t lead generation, but closing. The dashboard revealed where revenue slipped away, which channels delivered the most value, and who the top reps were — turning numbers into clear, actionable insights.
